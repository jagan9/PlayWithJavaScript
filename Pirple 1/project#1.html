Anna Karenina

A K by Leo Tolstoy is a tragic novel that follows the rise and fall
of the love affair between Anna Karenina and Count Vronsky. The main
premise of the eight-part novel as portrayed by its main characters such as
Dolly,Kitty and Stiva is the conflict between human desires and societal roles.
